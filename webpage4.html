Who is SEVENTEEN?

SEVENTEEN is a South Korean boy group under Pledis Entertainment, debuted on May 26, 2015. They are known as the "self-producing idols" because they produce all of their songs, choreograph their own dances, and participate in making their own concepts. They also produce their own magazine and even made their own MVs (Snapshoot & Holiday). They consist of 13 members divided into three units: Hip-Hop Team (S.Coups, Wonwoo, Mingyu, and Vernon), Performance Team (Hoshi, Jun, The8, and Dino), and Vocal Unit (Jeonghan, Joshua, Woozi, DK, and Seungkwan). They also produced four sub-units: BSS, JxW, HxW, and CxM.

Why are they called SEVENTEEN if they have 13 members?

The thirteen members are divided into three units but together they form one team — a simple equation that explains the group’s name: 13 + 3 + 1 = SEVENTEEN.
