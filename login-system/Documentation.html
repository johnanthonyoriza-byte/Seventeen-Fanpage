Documentation


Week 1

During the first week, our team started by planning and distributing the roles. We discussed our topic, concepts, and themes. We decided on picking the K-Pop Group Seventeen suggested by our fanatic member. After that, we gathered materials and took inspiration from other sources.



Week 2

In the 2nd week, we began coding and structuring our website, experimenting with different styles, layouts, and color themes to find the best design that fits our theme. We also explored various formatting options to make sure that our site looked clean, functional, and visually appealing.



Week 3

In the last few days, we focused on debugging and editing the issues that we may find and parts we weren’t satisfied with. We added some final touches to improve the overall design and functionality, and this became our finished product.
